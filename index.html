About me

My name is Leandro Rosa, I'm 28 years old, and I currently reside in Rio de Janeiro. Always passionate about technology. I've always dreamed of working as a programmer one day, and today I feel fulfilled to be able to study and interact daily with people who share the same passion as me. My first steps were as a computer technician in 2012, where I learned a lot about hardware and software (even back then, I dabbled in basic programming). Always seeking to gain knowledge in the field, I decided to invest all my time in studying to learn web development, and it was in this area that I fell in love at first sight! Today, I work in the military personnel systems department, at Almirante Tamandaré Building in downtown Rio de Janeiro, but I keep studying and specializing daily, always focusing on evolving and building a range of knowledge in this market that I am so passionate about!
